# Instant Gels™ — Creative Brief (condensed)

## Content pillars (what footage to expect / index)
1. **Application** — clean close-ups, **both methods** (adhesive tabs and glue), unboxing + product on display, before/afters. *Must be applied to healthy, natural nails.*
2. **Removal** — clean close-ups, both methods, healthy natural nails shown after.
3. **Photos + before/after short clips** for **every colour** launching (usual style).
4. **ASMR application reels** — per colour; fast, satisfying, sound-led. Inspiration: Bymeandcrew, Celebritips, BtartBox. Note specific shots/angles from top-viewed examples.
5. **Wear test / durability** — daily activities (typing, handwashing) + exaggerated tests (hammer, key, drill, screwdriver). Inspiration: Celebritips durability shorts (their top performers).
6. **High-production** photography + short clips for website/LP/social (16 product-page photos defined in the original).
7. **UGC** — B-roll, application, storyline, unboxing, street interviews, first-reaction.
8. **Shoot content** — lab-style educational, consumer-trial behind-the-scenes, salon, warehouse.
9. **Model shoots** — various hand skin tones + faces, editorial.

## Styling notes
- Aesthetic: bright, clean, modern; minimalist backgrounds (improve on usual style where possible).
- Manicure on **natural nails**.
- Show **final retail packaging only** — repack each colour into the approved box so every video shows correct packaging (correct file, alcohol wipe, glue).

## Competitors (benchmark to exceed)
Glamnetic (US, #1) · Doonails (EU) · Sassy Saints (UK) · BtartBox (US) · Nailboo (US) · Static Nails (US,
high production) · Celebritips (US, durability shorts) · Glowiebyher (AU) · Bymeandcrew (AU, ASMR).

## Channels
Website, landing pages, socials, email. Meta placements (9:16 Reels/Stories, 4:5 Feed, 1:1 carousel),
TikTok, YouTube Shorts.

## Volume context
32 styles × 3,000 units = ~96k sets (launch ~April 2026).
