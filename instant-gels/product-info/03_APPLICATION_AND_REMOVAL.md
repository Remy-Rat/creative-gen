# Instant Gels™ — Application & Removal (approved process)

> Defines the canonical **process-step vocabulary** the indexer tags against. Two methods: **tabs** and **glue**.
> Wordings outside this approved copy must go to Regulatory.

## Process-step vocabulary (controlled enum)
`prep_shape` · `prep_pushback` · `prep_buff` · `prep_cleanse` · `size_match` · `tab_apply` ·
`glue_apply` · `position_at_cuticle` · `press_hold` · `final_reveal` · `removal_soak` ·
`removal_lift` · `removal_cleanup` · `reuse_clean`
Plus a `method` axis: `tabs` | `glue` | `n/a`.

## Packaging quick-start (3 steps)
1. Shape, buff and cleanse natural nails using the prep kit.
2. Apply nail glue or adhesive tabs to natural nails, then press on the nails.
3. Press and hold ~30 seconds. Reusable and reapplicable after removal.

## Before you start
Wash hands with soap and water. Nails must be completely clean, dry and free from oils, lotion or residue
for best adhesion.

## Nail prep (`prep_*`)
1. Trim, file and shape natural nails as desired. *(prep_shape)*
2. Gently push back cuticles with the manicure stick. *(prep_pushback)*
3. Lightly buff each nail to remove shine. *(prep_buff)*
4. Clean nails with the prep wipe; allow to fully dry. *(prep_cleanse)*
> Tip: skipping prep may cause lifting or reduced wear time.

## Apply with adhesive tabs (`method: tabs`)
1. Match each press-on to your natural nail size; lay out in order (size numbers on the underside). *(size_match)*
2. Apply the tab to the natural nail, press firmly, smooth out air bubbles. *(tab_apply)*
3. Peel off the top film.
4. Position the nail close to the cuticle, press from the centre outward. *(position_at_cuticle)*
5. Hold firm pressure 20–30 seconds. *(press_hold)*
> Pro tip: note the tab size in your sizing guide for easy reapplication.

## Apply with glue (`method: glue`)
1. Select the correct nail per finger; arrange in order. *(size_match)*
2. Hold glue upright and open carefully.
3. Apply a thin, even layer of glue to the back of the press-on, then a small amount to the natural nail. *(glue_apply)*
4. Starting at the cuticle, place at a slight angle and lower onto the nail bed. *(position_at_cuticle)*
5. Press firmly without sliding; gentle pressure from the centre outward.
6. Hold 20–30 seconds until secure. *(press_hold)*
> Pro tip: enough glue to cover evenly, avoid excess near skin to prevent lifting.

## For best results
File sides if a nail is slightly wide · press down again to reinforce · extra centre pressure on curved
beds · reapply pressure if edges lift early · gloves for household tasks · keep tabs sealed · save leftover
nails for replacements/accents.

## Removal — adhesive tabs (`removal_*`)
1. Soak fingertips 3–5 min in warm water + a few drops of nail/cuticle oil (e.g. Heal Oil). *(removal_soak)*
2. Gently lift edges with a cuticle stick / fingertips, working from the sides. *(removal_lift)*
3. Don't force — soak longer if stuck (pulling can damage the natural nail).
4. Remove leftover adhesive from nails and press-ons; press-ons can be reused. *(removal_cleanup / reuse_clean)*

## Removal — glue (oil-soak method, recommended)
1. Apply cuticle/nail oil (e.g. Heal Oil) generously around cuticles, sides, under the free edge; massage. *(removal_soak)*
2. In a bowl mix warm water + ¼–½ tsp oil + 2 pumps liquid soap + 2–3 tbsp adhesive remover (e.g. Remove Solution).
3. Soak hands 5 min, moving fingers to loosen.
4. Slide a cuticle stick under the sides to create a small gap — do not force. *(removal_lift)*
5. Soak another 5 min, then gently lift off; repeat if resistance.
6. Wipe residue from natural nails with a little Remove Solution. *(removal_cleanup)*
7. Apply cuticle/nail oil to nourish natural nails and cuticles.
8. Remove glue residue from the back of each press-on (cuticle stick / gentle buff) to preserve for reuse. *(reuse_clean)*

### Quick removal tips
Never pull or force · trim length first to reduce tension if needed · keep soaking until they loosen naturally.
