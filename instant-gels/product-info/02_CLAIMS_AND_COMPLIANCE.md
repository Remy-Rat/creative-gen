# COMPLIANCE & SAFETY (mandatory in ads)

### Creative non-negotiables
- Nails must be shown applied to **healthy, natural nails**, and removal must show **healthy nails after**.
- Show **final retail packaging only** (correct file, alcohol wipe, glue).

### `safety_sensitive` triggers (auto-insert disclaimer)
Flag any clip that shows: glue near skin/eyes, glue application, children, or the prep pad near heat/flame.
When such a clip is used, the assembler must auto-insert the relevant verbatim caution below.

### Nail Adhesive Glue — verbatim caution (do not paraphrase)
> Contains ethyl-2-cyanoacrylate. Cyanoacrylate. **Danger. Bonds skin and eyes in seconds.** Keep out of
> the reach of children. Avoid contact with eyes, mouth and skin. Eyelid bonding: consult a physician. Skin
> bonding: soak and ease apart gently. Not for use in the area of the eye. Causes skin irritation. Causes
> serious eye irritation. May cause respiratory irritation. Use only outdoors or in a well-ventilated area.
> If medical advice is needed, have product container or label at hand. Store locked up. Dispose of
> contents/container in accordance with local and national regulations.

### Adhesive Tabs — caution
> For nail use only. Do not apply directly to skin. Keep out of reach of children.

### Prep Pad — caution
> For external, cosmetic use only. Keep out of reach of children. Flammable — keep away from heat, sparks,
> open flame and hot surfaces.

### Ingredients (for reference)
- Nailed It Glue: Ethyl 2-Cyanoacrylate, Polymethyl Methacrylate.
- Adhesive Tab: Acrylates Copolymer, Polyethylene Terephthalate.
- Prep Pad: Isopropyl Alcohol, Water.
