# Instant Gels™ — Clinical Trial Capture Brief (condensed)

> Authentic, documentary-style content from the efficacy trial, to back specific claims. v1.1, June 2026.
> Face-free throughout. (Polished product imagery is produced separately.)

## Non-negotiables
- **No identifiable faces** — hands, nails, forearms only.
- **Consent first** — only participants who agreed to marketing capture at recruitment.
- **Nothing legible we didn't supply** — no Polish forms/labels/screens, no other brands in frame.
- **Clean, uncluttered frames.**

## Filming basics
Phone is fine (clean lens, 4K, lock focus/exposure, slow-mo for timed close-ups). Capture both **9:16 and
16:9** with breathing room to crop. One soft even light (no warm/cool mix). Clean white/grey bench. Brace
for close-ups; move closer rather than zoom. Send originals unedited; name `GLAMRDIP_[stage]_[shot]_[anonID]_[orientation]`
(wear shots add timepoint, e.g. `_Day14`).

## Shots (each ties to a claim)
**A · Application visit**
- A1 — full application of one nail, real time, **timer visible**; top-down + 3/4 side. *(application-time claim)*
- A2 — before/after photo pair, same hand/framing/light. *(finish quality)*
- A3 — gloved-hands application detail. *(clinical credibility)*

**B · Wear & durability**
- B1 — wear checkpoint close-up, same framing each visit (timepoint in filename). *(wear-time)*
- B2 — edge & cuticle macro, no lifting/chipping. *(durability)*

**C · Removal visit**
- C1 — removal real time, **timer visible**, branded remover bowl; top-down + 3/4. *(removal-time)*
- C2 — natural nail immediately after removal. *(gentle / no-damage)*

**D · Clinical setting B-roll**
- D1 — assessor measuring nails with caliper/ruler (hands only). *(clinically tested)*
- D2 — hand completing branded English questionnaire on clipboard. *(would use / recommend)*
- D3 — workstation flat-lay, no people. *(clinical scene-set)*

## Props Glamrdip supplies
Branded remover bowls · branded English-language forms · Instant Gels packaging + finished units for B-roll.
