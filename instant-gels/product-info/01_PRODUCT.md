# Instant Gels™ — Product Reference

> Glamrdip's second product line. Press-on nails with a gel finish. **Distinct from the dip-powder
> system** — positioned as *instant, swappable, fashion-led*, where dip is *build-your-own ritual*.

## What it is
**Instant Gels™** are pre-cured press-on nails with a gel-like finish, giving salon-quality nails at
home. Each nail is made with **GelStack™ Technology** — a multi-layer system of soft, flexible gel that
creates a smooth, builder-gel-inspired look. All layers are fully cured before packaging, so **no UV, LED
or chemical curing** is needed. Apply with glue or an adhesive tab, press and go. Swap styles midweek,
go bold on weekends, keep it polished for work.

## GelStack™ Technology — 6-layer pre-cured system
Fully set before packaging (no curing by the user), for consistent shape, durability and strength.
1. Gel tip
2. Base coat
3. Colour gel
4. Pattern / design
5. Building gel finish
6. Top coat

## What's in the kit
- 32 nails in 16 inclusive sizes (size numbers printed on the underside of each nail)
- **Nailed It Glue** (net wt. 0.07 oz / 2 ml)
- 32 adhesive tabs
- **Smooth Moves** double-sided nail file
- Cuticle stick
- 2 × prep pads

## Key features
- Inclusive sizing — 32 nails / 16 sizes
- Customisable — mix nails across sets for unique looks
- Reusable (with care)
- U-shape **Zero Edge** fit
- Easy application and removal
- Durable, chip-resistant, salon-style builder-gel finish
- Packaging: FSC-certified paper + 100% post-consumer recycled PET, fully recyclable

## Design innovations
- **Zero Edge Flexi-Fit** edges — stronger adhesion, less lifting and snagging
- **0-thickness cuticle base** — seamless gel-like finish along the edge and cuticle
- **APEX shape** — natural, seamless look

### Vs standard press-ons
| Standard press-ons | Instant Gels™ |
|---|---|
| Uniform thickness, visible cuticle edge | 0-thickness cuticle base, seamless line |
| Edges lift, snag, feel less natural | Zero Edge Flexi-Fit, snag-resistant |
| Often need filing/adjustment | Precision sculpted, natural contour fit |
| Stiff, bulky | Soft-flex, lightweight, moves with the nail |
